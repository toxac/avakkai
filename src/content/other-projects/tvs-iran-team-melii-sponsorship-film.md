## Brief

TVS Iran, as a proud sponsor of Team Melli (the Iranian national football team), needed a campaign that would connect with fans on a deeply emotional and cultural level. The objective was to celebrate the sponsorship through comedy — tapping into the universal madness of football fandom and the superstitions fans swear by. The challenge: create two films with a single shoot in Bangalore, featuring Iranian talent, with comedy that would land authentically despite the language gap.

## Our Concept & Approach

Avakkai Studio leaned into a universal truth: every football fan has a ritual they swear works — a seat, a shirt, a silence at the right moment. The campaign leans straight into that particular madness, following just how far belief can be stretched in the name of a win.

**The Core Idea:** Football superstition, taken all the way. Two films, two setups, one shared truth: fans will do anything for the team they love.

**Production & Creative Approach:**
- **Iranian Talent:** Casting genuinely Iranian talent was essential for authenticity
- **Two-Day Workshop:** Before the shoot, two days of workshopping with the cast — not rehearsing lines, but building rhythm, timing, and instinct together
- **Language Bridge:** The workshops allowed humour to stop being translated and start being organic, ensuring comic timing landed naturally on camera
- **Single Shoot:** Two films, two setups, came together on a single Bangalore shoot with no compromise on quality or comedy

## Outcome & Results

The campaign delivered authentic, universally relatable comedy that transcended cultural and linguistic barriers:

- **Universal Fan Insight:** The superstition is universal — every fan everywhere has one — which made an Iranian cast, shot in Bangalore, land as authentic rather than approximated

- **Language No Barrier:** With enough prep and enough belief, the language barrier disappeared — what's left is just a fan doing something a little unhinged for the team he loves

- **Workshop-Driven Success:** Two days of workshopping ensured the comedy stopped being translated and started being organic, with rhythm and timing built through instinct rather than direction

- **Efficient Production:** Two films, two setups, completed on a single Bangalore shoot — demonstrating Avakkai Studio's ability to deliver complex comedy campaigns within tight production windows

- **Brand Connection:** TVS Iran's sponsorship of Team Melli was celebrated through a campaign that felt deeply connected to fan culture, positioning the brand as one that truly understands its audience
