## Brief

AbhiBus, one of India's leading online bus ticketing (OTA) platforms, needed to build brand trust, expand market reach, and drive user acquisition. The objective was to create a high-impact launch campaign that would position AbhiBus as the most convenient and reliable way to book bus tickets. To achieve mass appeal and instant credibility, the brand onboarded Telugu cinema superstar Mahesh Babu as their brand ambassador — requiring a celebrity-driven campaign that could deliver on both scale and creative impact.

## Our Concept & Approach

Avakkai Studio developed a three-film campaign built on a simple yet powerful promise: booking a bus ticket should be this easy, all year round. The creative strategy leveraged Mahesh Babu's natural screen presence and comedic timing to tell humour-led stories, each finding its own comic angle around the same core idea — effortless travel with AbhiBus, regardless of season or reason.

To execute within a demanding single-day shoot schedule at Ramoji Film City, we employed a controlled studio setup featuring:
- A single actual bus mounted on motorised rigging to simulate motion
- A green chroma floor for flexible post-production enhancements
- Precise pre-planning to capture three full films plus a teaser with minimal retakes

This approach allowed us to maximize production value while working within the constraints of a celebrity's limited availability.

## Outcome & Results

The campaign successfully delivered on all objectives:
- **Mass Reach & Credibility:** Mahesh Babu's star power instantly elevated brand trust and recognition across Telugu-speaking markets
- **Creative Efficiency:** All three films and teaser were delivered on schedule and on brief, despite the intense single-day production timeline
- **Brand Positioning:** The humour-led narrative effectively communicated AbhiBus's value proposition — convenience, reliability, and ease of use
- **Production Excellence:** The innovative use of motorised rigging and chroma-floor techniques proved that high-production value could be achieved within tight schedules

The campaign gave AbhiBus a strong launchpad to build awareness, drive user adoption, and compete confidently in India's crowded OTA market.
