## Brief

Avakkai Studio produced a Three-film celebrity TVC campaign featuring Telugu cinema superstar Mahesh Babu for AbhiBus, India's leading online bus ticketing platform. The high-production commercials leveraged Mahesh Babu's mass appeal and trusted screen presence to establish brand credibility, drive user acquisition, and position AbhiBus as the go-to OTA for hassle-free bus travel. Shot in a single day at Ramoji Film City with in indoor green chroma- studio floor , the campaign delivered three comedy-led films and a teaser that seamlessly blended star power with creative storytelling.

## Our Concept & Approach

Avakkai Studio along with JWT Hyderabad  developed a three-film campaign built on a simple yet powerful promise: booking a bus ticket should be this easy, all year round. The creative strategy leveraged Mahesh Babu’s natural screen presence and comedic timing to tell humour-led stories, each finding its own comic angle around the same core idea — effortless travel with AbhiBus, regardless of season or reason.

To execute a demanding single-day shoot schedule at Ramoji Film City, we employed a controlled studio setup featuring:
- A single actual bus mounted on motorised rigging to simulate motion with multicamera set up rigged inside the bus.
- A green chroma floor for flexible post-production enhancements
- Precise pre-planning to capture three tvc’s, 1 teaser with minimal retakes

This approach allowed us to maximize production value while working within the constraints of a celebrity’s limited availability.

## Outcome & Results

The campaign successfully delivered on all objectives:
- **Mass Reach & Credibility:** Mahesh Babu's star power instantly elevated brand trust and recognition across Telugu-speaking markets
- **Creative Efficiency:** All three films and teaser were delivered on schedule and on brief, despite the intense single-day production timeline
- **Brand Positioning:** The humour-led narrative effectively communicated AbhiBus's value proposition — convenience, reliability, and ease of use
- **Production Excellence:** The innovative use of motorised rigging and chroma-floor techniques proved that high-production value could be achieved within tight schedules

The campaign gave AbhiBus a strong launchpad to build awareness, drive user adoption, and compete confidently in India's crowded OTA market.
