## Brief

Tata Consumer Products, following the success of Tata Gluco Jelly's launch campaign, needed a film for Tata Gluco+ that would stand out in the crowded energy drink category. The creative concept, developed by Wunderman Thompson, Delhi, was ambitious and satirical: a boat tour selling the impossible — the discovery of a hidden temple beneath the ocean.

The challenge was tonal. A concept this big — churning oceans, sunken temples, a satirical tour gone sideways — could easily tip into slapstick. The brief was to hold the comedy in the premise and the punchline, not in mugging performances or broad gags.

## Our Concept & Approach

Avakkai Studio reimagined the ancient myth of Samudra Manthan — the churning of the ocean by gods and demons — as a self-aware, satirical advertising comedy. The film is built as a tall tale, deliberately knowing, so the payoff lands as a wink rather than a lecture.

**The Core Idea:** A satirical boat tour selling something no tour should be able to deliver. The boat conks off mid-promise. Enter the surfers: they down Tata Gluco+, paddle out, and physically churn the ocean into waves powerful enough to bring the temple up from the deep. Myth, tourism-spoof, and product energy, all folded into one fantasy.

**Production Highlights:**
- **Real Location:** Shot on Pondicherry's coastline, grounding the fantasy in a believable, tangible reality
- **Practical Base:** A real location, real surf team, and real ocean did the grounding before VFX did the lifting
- **Boat Redesign:** A 100-passenger boat was redesigned and repurposed as a set, carrying the cast and creating the film's primary location
- **International Talent:** An international surf team brought authenticity and athleticism to the churning sequences
- **VFX by Poland:** The temple rising and the ocean churning were elevated by a Polish VFX studio, ensuring the fantasy felt cinematic, not cartoonish
- **Tonal Discipline:** The surfers played it straight — the absurdity did the work on its own

## Outcome & Results

The campaign delivered exceptional creative impact, proving that big ideas and disciplined execution create memorable advertising:

- **Myth Reimagined:** The film repurposed the Samudra Manthan myth as a punchline, making it culturally resonant while remaining fresh and surprising for modern audiences

- **Perfect Tonal Balance:** By holding the comedy in the premise and punchline rather than broad performances, the film avoided slapstick — landing as clever, self-aware satire

- **Production Excellence:** The integration of real locations, international talent, practical stunts, and international VFX created a seamless fantasy world that felt believable enough to be funny, not cartoonish

- **Earned the Punchline:** The entire film was built to deliver the payoff: *"This story has a lot of gas, but Tata Gluco+ is Gas Minus, Energy Plus."* The tale is deliberately, knowingly tall, so the punchline lands as a wink rather than a lecture

- **Visual Spectacle:** The combination of Pondicherry's coastline, a redesigned 100-passenger boat, an international surf team, and Polish VFX created a cinematic experience that elevated the brand's advertising standards

- **Brand Positioning:** Tata Gluco+ was positioned as the energy drink that can power the impossible — even churning the ocean itself
