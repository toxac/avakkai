## Brief

TVS Motor Company needed a portfolio of broadcast-quality launch films, anniversary campaigns, and seasonal content for the African and Egyptian markets — films that would feel authentically local, not like Indian productions dubbed for export. The challenge: deliver multiple distinct campaigns across five markets, each with its own cultural nuance, without the budget or timeline of a multi-continent production.

## Our Concept & Approach

Avakkai Studio approached the portfolio with a single principle: international campaigns don't require international production budgets. They require production intelligence — knowing how to source, cast, and execute across cultural contexts without losing quality.

**The Portfolio Included:**
- **TVS Neo AMI Launch** — Africa market entry film for Gen Z
- **10 Years of HLX in Africa** — Brand anniversary celebration
- **TVS HLX 150F Launch** — Egypt market premium bike
- **TVS HLX Eid Mubarak** — Africa seasonal campaign
- **Team Melli — Superstitious Fan** — Iran culturally tailored comedy

**Production & Technical Approach:**
- **Regional Talent Casting:** Each film used talent sourced from the target region, ensuring authentic cultural representation
- **Location Scouting & Recreation:** Visual language matched each market through strategic location scouting or in-studio recreation
- **14-Foot LED Wall:** Multiple films were shot against a large LED wall in Bangalore, allowing complete control over environments without leaving India
- **Single Coordinated Team:** What looked like a multi-continent production was delivered from a single, tightly coordinated production team

## Outcome & Results

The portfolio demonstrated that production intelligence can deliver authentic international campaigns from a single base:

- **Authentic Local Feel:** Through strategic casting and location recreation, each film felt native to its market — resonating with local audiences despite being produced in India

- **Production Efficiency:** A single coordinated team delivered multiple campaigns across five markets, proving that international production doesn't require international budgets

- **Cultural Nuance:** From Iran's football comedy to Africa's Gen Z tech energy, each campaign was tailored to its audience's cultural context

- **Direct-to-Client Trust:** TVS trusted Avakkai Studio with five distinct markets — and every one was delivered on brief, on quality, on time

- **Portfolio Cohesion:** Despite the diversity of markets and campaigns, the work maintained a consistent TVS brand thread, ensuring the parent brand remained recognisable across regions
